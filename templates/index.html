Popular University Cafeteria Foods

Discover Our Delicious Offerings

  • Bhaji with Paratha

Biriyani

Aromatic rice dish cooked with flavorful spices and tender pieces of meat or vegetables.

Biriyani is a beloved Bangladeshi delicacy known for its rich taste and fragrant aroma. It's a must-try dish in our university cafeteria.

Hilsa Fish Curry

A traditional Bangladeshi fish curry made with fresh hilsa fish and aromatic spices.

Hilsa Fish Curry is a quintessential Bengali dish, and our cafeteria serves it with perfection. Experience the flavors of Bangladesh!

Fuchka (Pani Puri)

A popular street food snack consisting of hollow crispy balls filled with tangy tamarind water and spicy stuffing.

Indulge in the delightful flavors of Fuchka, a beloved street food from Bangladesh, right here in our cafeteria. Taste the excitement!

Bharta with Paratha

Vegetables (bhaji) served with flaky paratha bread.

Experience the comfort of Bhaji with Paratha, a classic Bangladeshi combination that's both hearty and delicious.

Chingri Malai Curry

A creamy coconut-based shrimp curry, a Bengali culinary masterpiece.

Indulge in the exquisite flavors of Chingri Malai Curry, a culinary masterpiece from Bangladesh, right here in our cafeteria.
